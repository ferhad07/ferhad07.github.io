01 — Why SafeRoots

Climate change is a human crisis—not only an environmental one.

It disrupts children’s safety, education, health, identity, and sense of home. Yet climate and protection systems are too often designed separately.

SafeRoots is being built to close that gap: combining science with local knowledge, and practical adaptation with dignity, participation, and care.

05 — From plan to practice

A responsible path to action.

Ambition matters. Sequence, safeguarding, and proof matter more.

01Foundation

Build the institution safely

Complete legal registration, independent governance, safeguarding standards, and transparent financial controls.

02Listening

Design with communities

Form ethical research partnerships and involve children, youth, caregivers, and local organizations in decisions.

03Pilot

Test integrated programs

Start small with climate-resilient schools, Eco-PSS, and community risk mapping—then publish what we learn.

04Scale

Grow through evidence

Expand only where local ownership, safeguarding capacity, funding, and independent evidence support it.

Founding network

The strongest roots are built together.

We are building in public and welcome child-protection practitioners, climate scientists, educators, legal advisors, community organizations, and responsible funders.
